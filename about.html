Artistic Bio

Pria Dahiya makes theater, videos, dance, and performance art about the overwhelming stream of the constant now. Her projects reek with vulnerability and curdle with satire.

She investigates digital identity, internet culture, cringe, surveillance capitalism, and tender love through multidisciplinary experimentation. She has spent twenty-four years being biracial, bisexual, and chronically online, and her work touches on how the internet can hide, broadcast, and manipulate these conflicting identities.

She is an artistic director of Pittsburgh-based experimetal theatre company New Product Company and has written criticism for American Theatre Magazine. She has shown work at Fugue Gallery (NYC), Kelly Strayhorn Theatre (PGH), and the Mattress Factory (PGH).

BFA from the John Wells Directing Program at Carnegie Mellon School of Drama. Based between New York, Philadelphia and Pittsburgh.
